Sentimental Analysis Using Twitter Data - A Lexion based Approach Sentiment analysis using Twitter data via a lexicon-based approach is an unsupervised Natural Language Processing (NLP) technique that determines the emotional polarity (positive, negative, or neutral) of tweets by matching words within them against a pre-compiled, sentiment-annotated dictionary (lexicon). Unlike machine learning, this method does not require training data; instead, it relies on calculated scores assigned to words to determine the overall sentiment of a tweet. Natural Language Processing, Tokenization, SVM, Decision Tree and Logistic Regression
